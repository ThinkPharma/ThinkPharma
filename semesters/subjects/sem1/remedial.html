📘 Remedial Biology - PCI Syllabus

Unit 1

Living World & Diversity

9 Hours
  • Characteristics of living organisms
  • Classification of living organisms
  • Five kingdom classification
  • Binomial nomenclature
  • Salient features of each kingdom
Unit 2

Morphology of Plants

9 Hours
  • Root system - types and modifications
  • Stem - types and modifications
  • Leaf - types, venation, phyllotaxy
  • Flower - parts and functions
  • Fruit and seed - types and dispersal
Unit 3

Cell Biology

9 Hours
  • Cell theory and cell types
  • Cell organelles and their functions
  • Prokaryotic vs Eukaryotic cells
  • Cell division - Mitosis and Meiosis
  • Chromosomes and their structure
Unit 4

Animal Tissues

9 Hours
  • Epithelial tissue - types and functions
  • Connective tissue - types and functions
  • Muscular tissue - types and functions
  • Nervous tissue - structure and function
  • Location and role of each tissue type
Unit 5

Plant Physiology & Genetics

9 Hours
  • Photosynthesis - process and importance
  • Respiration in plants
  • Transport in plants
  • Introduction to genetics
  • Mendel's laws of inheritance

📘 Remedial Mathematics - PCI Syllabus

Unit 1

Partial Fractions & Logarithms

9 Hours
  • Partial fractions - introduction and types
  • Linear factors and repeated factors
  • Quadratic factors
  • Logarithms - definition and laws
  • Common and natural logarithms
Unit 2

Matrices & Determinants

9 Hours
  • Introduction to matrices
  • Types of matrices
  • Matrix operations - addition, multiplication
  • Determinants - evaluation
  • Cramer's rule for solving equations
Unit 3

Trigonometry

9 Hours
  • Trigonometric ratios and identities
  • Compound angles
  • Multiple and submultiple angles
  • Inverse trigonometric functions
  • Solutions of triangles
Unit 4

Analytical Geometry

9 Hours
  • Straight lines - various forms
  • Angle between two lines
  • Circle - equation and properties
  • Parabola - equation and properties
  • Applications in pharmacy
Unit 5

Calculus

9 Hours
  • Limits and continuity
  • Differentiation - basic rules
  • Differentiation of standard functions
  • Integration - basic rules
  • Integration of standard functions
