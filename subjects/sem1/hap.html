Human Anatomy & Physiology I

Theory | 4 Credits | 45 Hours

5 Units
Notes Available
PYQs Available

📘 PCI Syllabus

Unit 1

Introduction to Human Body

10 Hours
  • Definition and scope of anatomy and physiology
  • Levels of structural organization
  • Basic life processes
  • Homeostasis and feedback mechanisms
  • Anatomical terminology
Unit 2

Cellular Level of Organization

8 Hours
  • Structure and components of cell
  • Cell membrane and transport mechanisms
  • Cell division - Mitosis and Meiosis
  • Cellular organelles and their functions
  • Cell cycle and its regulation
Unit 3

Tissues

9 Hours
  • Classification of tissues
  • Epithelial tissues - types and functions
  • Connective tissues - types and functions
  • Muscular tissues - types and functions
  • Nervous tissues - structure and functions
Unit 4

Skeletal System

10 Hours
  • Bone tissue and bone formation
  • Classification of bones
  • Axial skeleton - skull, vertebral column, thoracic cage
  • Appendicular skeleton - limbs and girdles
  • Joints - classification and movements
Unit 5

Muscular System & Digestive System

8 Hours
  • Types of muscles and their properties
  • Mechanism of muscle contraction
  • Digestive system - organs and accessory organs
  • Digestion and absorption process
  • Gastrointestinal hormones
